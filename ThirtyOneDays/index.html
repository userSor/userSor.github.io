Points System

Points are calculated by adding the points of each item you have.

Each item is worth:

  1. Materials: 1 points
  2. Bandages: 2 points
  3. Food: 1 points
  4. Water: 2 points
  5. Antidotes: 3 points
  6. Each Rifle Weapon Level: 10 points
